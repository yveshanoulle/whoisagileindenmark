After the growing success of the global [“Who is agile?”](https://leanpub.com/WhoIsAgile) book, it has been decided to go for local country editions. This book is an example of such edition and is not the only one.

Already published local country versions, apart from this one:

* [“Who is agile in Australia & New Zealand?”](https://leanpub.com/whoisagileAustralia) Local team: Sunish Chabba, Venkatesh Krishnamurthy, and Renee Troughton.
* [“Who is agile in India”](https://leanpub.com/whoisagileinindia) Local team: Sunish Chabba and Tushar Somaiya.
* [“Who is agile in Nigeria”](https://leanpub.com/WhoIsAgileInNigeria) Local team: Chinyere Oranugo, Emmanuel Umoke, Nnamdi C, Dorcas Sina-Olulana, Bassey Bassey, Kosisochukwu H Abone, OGHENETEJIRI OKPALEFE and Blessing Shelika.
* [“Who is agile in Ukraine?”](https://leanpub.com/WhoisagileinUkraine) Local team: Kirill Klimov.
* [“Who is agile in Singapore?”](https://leanpub.com/whoisagileinsingapore) Local team: Kirill Klimov.
* [“Who is agile in South Africa?”](https://leanpub.com/WhoisagileSouthAfrica) Local team: Bevan Williams, Yogini Moodley, Nkululeko Masondo, Nilesh Makan, Samantha Laing, Karen Greaves, and Suwilo


There is now also the video edition, that can be found on YouTube: https://bit.ly/WhoIsagile
