#How to give feedback? {#FeedBack}


We are eager to hear back from you, dear reader. Please, let us know what you think about the book, what you liked and how you think it could become better. The book is still under development, so your feedback could help us to make it better. Please, leave your feedback, comments, ideas as well as error reports on [LeanPub page](https://leanpub.com/WhoisagileinDenmark/feedback).

<!--Please, add feedback to our [GoodReads page](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/29366420-who-is-agile-in-ukraine).-->

If you would like to write to somebody from the book, please, refer to [contacts section](#Contacts).

Last, but not least. Please, help us spread the word about this book by [tweeting about it](https://twitter.com/intent/tweet?text=Just%20read%20a%20book%20%22Who%20is%20agile%20in%20Denmark%22!%20%23WhoIsAgile). You can find out what other people are saying about the book by using its hashtag on Twitter: [#WhoIsAgile](https://twitter.com/search?q=%23WhoIsAgile).
